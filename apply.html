Viewing Representation Assistance

As a result of the current housing crisis, many international students are unable to travel to the Netherlands prior to their studies and consequently miss out on physical viewings.

We offer to become your representative in Tilburg: we can attend housing viewings on your behalf, ask questions, inspect the property, and help raise your chances of securing permanent accommodation while avoiding online scammers.
