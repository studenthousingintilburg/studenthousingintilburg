Welcome to Student Housing in Tilburg (SHT). By accessing or using our platform, application forms, or matching services, you agree to comply with and be bound by these General Terms and Conditions.

1. Nature of the Service

SHT is a non-profit mutual aid initiative connecting students seeking temporary emergency accommodation with volunteer hosts. SHT does not act as a commercial real estate agent or landlord.

2. Liability Disclaimer

SHT is not liable for any damages, personal injury, financial disputes, or property loss arising out of agreements made between hosts and guests.

3. Duration & Compensation

Hosting arrangements facilitated by SHT are temporary (up to a maximum of 3 months). No formal rent may be charged through SHT, though hosts may request modest compensation for direct utility/grocery costs agreed upon in advance.

Student Housing in Tilburg is a non-profit mutual aid initiative dedicated to helping international and Dutch students find emergency housing.
